PEOPLE

Some (now dead) thinkers I admire.

  • Alan Turing (here or here): code-breaker, founder of computer science, honest man
  • Archimedes: citizen of Syracuse probably killed by a Roman soldier because he wanted to finish a proof
  • Pierre de Fermat: lawyer and parliamentarian who did maths for the fun of it, setter of puzzles
  • Evariste Galois: political hothead, duellist over love
  • Marie-Sophie Germain and Srinivasa Aiyangar Ramanujan: autodidacts
  • Andre Weil: conscientious objector ("My mathematics work is proceeding beyond my wildest hopes, and I am even a bit worried - if it is only in prison that I work so well, will I have to arrange to spend two or three months locked up every year?"), Poldavian
  • Desiderius Erasmus: humanist, theologian, classical scholar
